
The infographic provides an overview of the global and Indian Amines market, highlighting key growth drivers and regional trends. Globally, the Amines market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 3.7% from 2021 to 2027, with the market size expected to reach >$30 billion by 2027 from >$17 billion in 2020. This growth is fueled by several factors, including increased prevalence of chronic diseases requiring amine-based drugs, rising use of agrochemicals for crop protection, and expanding personal care and cosmetics industries utilizing amine-based ingredients. Regionally, the Asia-Pacific market is expected to witness the fastest growth at a CAGR of 9.2% from 2021 to 2027, followed by Europe at 8.4%. Within the global Amines market, Ethyl Amines hold the largest share at 11.5%, followed by Alkyl Amines (11%) and Methyl Amines (10.5%). Personal care applications account for 8.3% of the global market. While China dominates global amine production, recent environmental regulations have led to shutdowns, creating a supply-demand mismatch and presenting opportunities for Indian manufacturers.

The global Amines market is poised for steady growth, driven by increasing demand from pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and personal care industries.
